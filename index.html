Meet Jacob

For nearly a decade, Jacob Elyachar has become an influential content creator that specializes in feature and news writing, public relations, and social media. His work has been featured on various media outlets such as AXS, Comic Book Resources, The Kansas City Jewish Chronicle, Music Times, PopWrapped, and Tech Times. In addition to his passion of content creation, he loves giving back to his community. Over the years, Jacob volunteered and worked for various non-profit organizations including Americans For Older Driver Safety, the Freelance Exchange of Kansas City, the Jewish Federation of Greater Kansas City, Minddrive, and the UJA-Federation of New York City.
